The Yummiest Lemon Bars

Lemons bars are the greatest on dessert on Earth. If you have a lemon tree, making lemons bars is the best way to use all of those lemons. This recipe make the perfect lemon bars.

Photo credit: Joy Of Baking

  • 1 cup of unbleached flour
  • 1/3 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of water
  • 3 cups of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 Tablespoons of grated lemon peel
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 8x8 pan

Make the Lemon Bars

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine flour, butter and water in bowl. Mix until well combined.
  3. Press flour mixture into an 8X8 pan. Be sure to make an even crust.
  4. Bake flour mixture at 350 degrees for 15 minutes or until slightly golden brown
  5. While flour mixture is baking,in a separate bowl mix lemon juice, eggs and
  6. Pull baked crust from oven and pour lemon mixture over the warm crust
  7. Bake for an additional 15 minutes or until slightly brown.
  8. Pull pan from oven and let cool completely.
  9. Dust 1/4 cup of powdered sugar over the lemon bars. Serve and Enjoy!
